## Selection Criteria / 選擇標準

We rank venues against four priorities that are specific to a **positional paper + embedded prospective pilot** deliverable:

1. **Scope match for closed-loop preventive BCI** — does the journal publish work that pairs a prediction model with a clinical-outcome endpoint (not just offline AUC)?
2. **Receptivity to positional / perspective / pilot studies** — some high-impact venues require multi-site RCTs; others explicitly welcome pilot + roadmap hybrids.
3. **Priming by existing citations in our shortlist** — if the Step 3 shortlist already contains multiple papers published at venue X, editors and reviewers are calibrated for this topic.
4. **Impact-vs-accessibility balance** — we list one aspirational (IF >20), two moderate (IF 6–15), one BCI-native (IF 3–5), and one methodology-rigor venue.

我們按四個與**立場論文 + 內嵌前瞻性 pilot** 這個組合特別相關的優先順序排序：

1. **預測—預防閉環的領域吻合度** — 期刊是否刊登將預測模型與臨床結局（而非僅 offline AUC）配對的研究？
2. **對立場／觀點／pilot 研究的接受度** — 部分高 IF 期刊僅接受多中心 RCT；部分明確歡迎 pilot + roadmap 混合體。
3. **既有引用的暖身效應** — 若 Step 3 shortlist 已有多篇該期刊論文，編輯與審稿人對此主題的校準度高。
4. **衝擊與可達性的平衡** — 我們列出 1 本挑戰型（IF >20）、2 本中段（IF 6–15）、1 本 BCI 原生（IF 3–5）、1 本方法學嚴謹型期刊。

### Why Not Start at NBME / 為何不從 NBME 起跳

Nature Biomed Eng has highest IF but **requires a demonstrated clinical-significance effect**. A within-subject pilot with N=20 and rate-ratio 0.75 threshold is unlikely to clear NBME's bar unless the effect is substantially larger than our pre-specified target. Submitting to NBME first risks a 60-day delay + desk reject, during which the paradigm-shift argument may be scooped by competing groups (given accelerating activity in 2025–2026 closed-loop seizure literature).

NBME 的 IF 最高，但**要求臨床有效性實質證實**。N=20 且目標 rate-ratio 為 0.75 的 within-subject pilot，除非實際效果遠超預設門檻，否則難以達到 NBME 的標準。先投 NBME 會面臨 60 天拖延加 desk reject 的風險，而此期間這個典範轉移論點可能被其他團隊搶先（鑑於 2025–2026 閉環癲癇文獻正加速積累）。

### Timing Considerations / 時程考量

- **Preregistration first (2026-05 to 2026-06)** — register the pilot protocol on OSF / ClinicalTrials.gov **before** running the intervention phase. This is a npj DM and JNE editorial preference.
- **Positional paper drafting can start in parallel** — the Step 9 manuscript writing sub-skill should begin immediately; the pilot results section can be left as a placeholder until the 16-week protocol concludes.
- **Expected submission window**: 2026-10 to 2027-02 (after 12-month pilot completion + 2 months analysis).

## Risk Notes on Journal Fit / 期刊契合度風險備註

1. **Positional-paper genre compression risk**: npj DM and NBME both have word-count ceilings (~5,000 words) that compress paradigm-shift arguments. JNE's Topical Review format (up to 12,000 words) is more forgiving for the positional contribution.
2. **Pre-registration gatekeeping**: npj DM's 2024 editorial policy explicitly prefers pre-registered clinical interventions. Failure to pre-register the pilot would downgrade our preferred pathway to Step B directly.
3. **Reviewer pool divergence**: The same paper will be read very differently by a npj DM digital-health reviewer (who cares about intervention fidelity + effect size) vs. a JNE BCI reviewer (who cares about signal-processing rigor + paradigm contribution). Cover letters must be **rewritten per venue**, not reused.
4. **Open-access embargo asymmetry**: If the paper is for a university thesis requiring immediate OA, npj DM and NBME are preferable over JNE and TBME (both of which are hybrid with embargo).
